STATEN ISLAND FERRY
Staten Island Ferry


The Staten Island Ferry operates between the St. George ferry terminal on Staten Island and the Whitehall Terminal in Lower Manhattan. The trip is about 25 minutes long and it is free. It moves 22 million passengers a year between the St. George ferry terminal on Staten Island and the Whitehall Terminal in Lower Manhattan.

TRAIN STATION
Staten Island Railway


MTA Staten Island Railway (SIR) service runs 24 hours daily between the St. George ferry terminal and Tottenville stations. At the St. George station, customers can make connections with the Staten Island Ferry service.

BUS
Bus


The Metropolitan Transit Authority (MTA) operates a number of bus routes that run to and from the St. George ferry terminal of the Staten Island Ferry. Bus service runs 24 hour daily.
