9/11 MEMORIAL
9/11 Memorial


Postcards is an outdoor sculpture in St Geoege Staten Island. It was built in 2004. It is a memorial honoring the 274 Staten Island residents killed in the 1993 World Trade Center bombing and September 11th Attacks of 2001.

St. Geoege Library


The St. George Library was opened on June 26, 1907. It is the largest library on Staten Island. The library has a Children's Room and a Teen Library. The library also has PC's for public use and a computer lab for computer classes.
